GameDev - Kraken Rampage!

Thu, Mar 17, 2016

A game I made for ludumDare33. Theme you are the monster. In this game you are a squid like creature that shits ink on humanity. The objective is to prevent the humans from bunking. I didn’t finish on time but the game is playable and funny.

Software I used was Godot Engine, Inkscape, and Spriter.
